# Maryville University AI Organization

Welcome to the **official Maryville University Artificial Intelligence (AI) GitHub organization**.
This organization serves as a **central resource for students** to explore and learn about **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**, **Machine Learning (ML)**, and **Data Science**. Our goal is to provide educational materials, tutorials, and examples that help students build knowledge and skills in these exciting fields.

---

## About This Resource

The Maryville University AI organization is designed as a **learning hub** for students at Maryville University and anyone interested in AI. Here, you can:
- Explore **educational projects** and example code
- Access **tutorials and workshops** for hands-on learning
- Discover **AI and ML concepts** through practical examples
- Learn from **faculty-led and curated resources**

Our mission is to make AI and Data Science accessible to all students while providing **quality learning materials** in one place.

---

## Learning Materials and Projects:

Some of the types of resources you can find in our repositories:
  
- **Machine Learning Projects:** Predictive models, data analysis pipelines, and algorithm demonstrations
- **Research Highlights:** Summaries and examples from faculty-led AI research projects

> Browse all projects: [Maryville University AI Repositories](https://github.com/orgs/MaryvilleUniversity-AI/repositories)

## How to Use This Resource

- **Explore Repositories:** Click through the projects to view tutorials and example code
- **Learn at Your Own Pace:** Use the materials to practice and understand AI and Data Science concepts
- **Reference Materials:** Ideal for coursework, personal learning, or preparing for AI-related competitions
